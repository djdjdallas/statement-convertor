# Privacy Policy for Statement Desk

**Last Updated: January 2025**

## Introduction

Statement Desk ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Google Workspace add-on and related services.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Email address, name, and Google account ID when you authenticate
- **Financial Data**: Bank statement PDFs and transaction data you upload for processing
- **Usage Data**: Features used, queries made to AI assistant, and preferences

### 2. Information from Google Services
When you authorize Statement Desk, we may access:
- **Google Drive**: To read PDF files you select for processing
- **Google Sheets**: To export processed data to your spreadsheets
- **Google Account**: Basic profile information for authentication

### 3. Automatically Collected Information
- **Log Data**: IP address, browser type, access times
- **Device Information**: Operating system, browser version
- **Usage Analytics**: Feature usage, processing times, error rates

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses
1. **Process Bank Statements**: Extract and analyze transaction data from PDFs
2. **Provide AI Services**: Power categorization, insights, and conversational queries
3. **Export Data**: Create and populate Google Sheets with processed data
4. **Generate Analytics**: Create financial reports and forecasts

### Secondary Uses
1. **Service Improvement**: Enhance accuracy and add new features
2. **Customer Support**: Respond to inquiries and resolve issues
3. **Security**: Detect and prevent fraudulent or illegal activities
4. **Compliance**: Meet legal and regulatory requirements

## Data Processing and AI

### Claude AI Integration
- We use Anthropic's Claude AI to enhance transaction processing
- Transaction data is sent to Claude API for categorization and analysis
- No personally identifiable information is stored by the AI service
- AI processing is ephemeral - data is not retained after processing

### Data Minimization
- We process only the minimum data necessary for functionality
- PDF files are processed in memory and not stored
- Transaction data is retained only as long as you maintain an account

## Data Storage and Security

### Security Measures
- **Encryption**: All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access, multi-factor authentication for staff
- **Infrastructure**: Hosted on secure cloud infrastructure (Vercel, Supabase)
- **Monitoring**: 24/7 security monitoring and intrusion detection

### Data Location
- Primary data stored in United States data centers
- Backup data may be replicated to other regions for reliability
- AI processing occurs in Anthropic's secure infrastructure

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We DO NOT:
- Sell your personal or financial data
- Share data with advertisers or data brokers
- Use your data for purposes other than providing our service

### We MAY Share Data:
1. **Service Providers**: 
   - Anthropic (AI processing)
   - Stripe (payment processing)
   - Analytics providers (anonymized data only)

2. **Legal Requirements**:
   - When required by law or valid legal process
   - To protect rights, property, or safety
   - To investigate fraud or security issues

3. **Business Transfers**:
   - In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale
   - You will be notified of any ownership change

## Google API Services User Data Policy

Statement Desk's use of Google user data complies with Google API Services User Data Policy:

### Limited Use Disclosure
Statement Desk's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the [Google API Services User Data Policy](https://developers.google.com/terms/api-services-user-data-policy), including the Limited Use requirements.

### Specific Google Permissions Used
- **Google Drive (read-only)**: Access PDF files you explicitly select
- **Google Sheets (read/write)**: Create and update spreadsheets for data export
- **Google Auth**: Authenticate and maintain your session

### We Only Access:
- Files you explicitly select or create through our interface
- No broad access to your Google Drive
- No access to personal emails or other Google services

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Control
- **View Your Data**: Access all processed transactions and insights
- **Export Your Data**: Download all data in standard formats
- **Delete Your Data**: Request complete deletion of your account and data
- **Modify Preferences**: Control AI features and data processing options

### Data Portability
- Export data in CSV, Excel, or Google Sheets format
- Standard formats ensure compatibility with other tools

### Opt-Out Options
- Disable AI processing (use traditional extraction only)
- Limit analytics and insights generation
- Restrict data sharing with service providers

## Data Retention

### Active Accounts
- Transaction data retained while account is active
- Chat history retained for 30 days
- Analytics data aggregated after 90 days

### Deleted Accounts
- All personal data deleted within 30 days of account closure
- Anonymized aggregate data may be retained for service improvement
- Backup systems purged within 90 days

## Children's Privacy

Statement Desk is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we discover data from a child under 18, we will promptly delete it.

## International Data Transfers

If you access Statement Desk from outside the United States:
- Your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States
- We use appropriate safeguards for international transfers
- You consent to such transfer by using our service
